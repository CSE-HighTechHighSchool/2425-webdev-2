About Venice

Discover the rich history and charm of the Floating City.

Historical Timeline

421 AD

The founding of Venice begins as refugees from mainland Italy settle on the lagoon islands.

812 AD

Venice becomes a refuge for people fleeing barbarian invasions, solidifying its independent identity.

1204

The Fourth Crusade leads to the establishment of the Venetian Republic's dominance in trade.

1797

Napoleon conquers Venice, ending the centuries-old Venetian Republic and integrating it into the Austrian Empire.

1866

Venice becomes part of the Kingdom of Italy after the Third Italian War of Independence.

2024

Venice continues to enchant visitors with its unique canals, architecture, and vibrant cultural scene.
